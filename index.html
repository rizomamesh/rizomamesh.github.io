Rizoma is a digital energy mesh that empowers your energy choices.

With Rizoma, individuals or businesses can efficiently trade energy with each other by working in a truly decentralized and organic energy ecosystem, where everyone can be a producer and consumer.

Rizoma uses a custom blockchain mesh protocol to effortlessly tap into IoT energy metering devices and digital storefronts, leveraging best-in-class order-matching algorithms for you to get the most of your energy exchange. It allows you to consume less energy, trade more efficiently and be a friend to the environment.

Take control of your energy source, unlock its boundless potential with us, and make a difference.
